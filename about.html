What is Elementle?

Elementle is a daily element guessing game where you challenge yourself to identify a mystery element. Each day brings a new element to discover, perfect for enhancing your understanding of the periodic table.

Elementle Story

As a passionate enthusiast of STEM and a fan of word games, I wanted to create something that blends both worlds. Inspired by Wordle's ability to turn simple guessing into a daily ritual, I envisioned a game where those seeking knowledge find it entertaining, and those seeking fun find it educational.

Thus, Elementle was born. Whether you're a devoted science lover or simply curious about the elements, you can always take a few moments each day to immerse yourself in a challenge and take away something valuable.
